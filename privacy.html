FilrAI

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 9, 2026

FilrAI is a macOS app for researching SEC filings, company disclosures, market-related feeds, and AI-assisted summaries. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be processed when you use FilrAI and how that information is handled.

Important update: Some FilrAI features use cloud-based AI model providers. When you use those features, prompts, filing excerpts, questions, and generated outputs may be transmitted to third-party AI services to provide responses. Third-party AI providers may use submitted data according to their own policies, including to improve or train models.

Information You Provide

Depending on the features you use, FilrAI may process information you enter into the app, including:

  • Searches, company names, ticker symbols, filing references, and research questions.
  • Prompts, notes, excerpts, or documents that you ask FilrAI to summarize or analyze.
  • A user-provided identifier, such as a user agent, used to access third-party data sources including the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC).
  • Your email address and message contents if you contact support.

Cloud AI Processing

FilrAI may send user prompts, selected filing content, context needed to answer a request, and generated outputs to cloud AI model providers. These providers process the information to generate summaries, explanations, or other AI-assisted responses, and may use submitted data according to their own policies, including to improve or train models.

Please do not submit sensitive personal information, account credentials, confidential business information, material non-public information, or private financial records unless you are comfortable with that information being processed by cloud services.

Third-Party Services and Data Sources

FilrAI may connect to third-party services, including:

  • SEC systems and other public filing or market-information sources.
  • Cloud AI model providers used to generate AI-assisted responses.
  • Email and hosting providers used to operate this website and respond to support requests.

Third-party services may process information according to their own terms and privacy practices.

Local Storage and Developer-Controlled Servers

FilrAI may store app settings, identifiers, cached filing data, notes, or preferences locally on your device. FilrAI does not require user accounts. Unless a feature states otherwise, FilrAI does not operate a developer-controlled account database for storing your research history.

When cloud AI features are used, relevant request content is transmitted outside your device to provide the feature. Support emails and website hosting logs may also be retained by the service providers used to operate support and the website.

How Information Is Used

Information is used to provide and improve app functionality, retrieve public filing data, generate AI-assisted responses, troubleshoot issues, respond to support requests, maintain security, and comply with legal obligations.

Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

FilrAI does not sell personal information and does not use advertising frameworks. If analytics, crash reporting, or telemetry are added in the future, this policy will be updated to describe what is collected and how it is used.

Retention

Local app data remains on your device until you delete it through the app, your operating system, or by uninstalling FilrAI. Support emails may be retained as needed to respond to requests and maintain business records. Cloud providers may retain request data according to their own policies and service configurations.

Security

FilrAI uses reasonable safeguards, including encrypted network connections where supported by third-party services. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so please use care when deciding what to submit.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as FilrAI features, vendors, or data practices change. The effective date above will be revised when material updates are made.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact: filr.support@gmail.com.
