Privacy Policy

SEC Connect does not require user accounts and does not collect or store personal data on external servers.

The app may require a user-provided identifier (such as a user agent) to access third-party data sources including the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC). This information is stored locally on the device and is only used to make requests to those services.

All data requests are made directly from the user’s device to the respective data providers. SEC Connect does not transmit or store this information on developer-controlled servers.

The app does not use tracking technologies, advertising frameworks, or analytics services.

This privacy policy may be updated prior to release or as features evolve.
