AI Disclosure

Effective date: May 9, 2026

FilrAI includes AI-assisted features that can help summarize filings, explain disclosures, compare company information, and answer research questions.

Use of Cloud Models

Some AI features use cloud-based model providers. When you use those features, FilrAI may send your prompt, relevant filing excerpts, contextual information, and generated outputs to third-party AI services so they can process the request.

AI Limitations

AI-generated responses may contain mistakes. They may omit important details, misread a filing, misunderstand context, or present information that is stale or incomplete. Always verify important information against original filings and reliable sources.

Do Not Submit Sensitive Information

Do not submit account credentials, private financial records, confidential company information, material non-public information, or sensitive personal information unless you are comfortable with that information being processed by cloud services.

Human Review

FilrAI is designed to assist research, not replace professional judgment. Use your own review and consult qualified professionals before making legal, tax, accounting, financial, or investment decisions.
